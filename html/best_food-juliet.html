my best food

my best food is okasi soup and hot fufu

ingredient of my best food

  • paim oil
  • dry fish
  • fresh meat
  • grinded okasi leaf
  • water leaf
  • maggi
  • salt
  • pepper

how to prepare my best food

  1. steam your dry fish with the meat
  2. add your pepper,salt,and maggi to the taste you want
  3. allow to boil for 15mins
